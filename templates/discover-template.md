# Phase 1: Discover (Business Discovery & Context)

## Goal
Understand the "why" from a business perspective.

## Key Questions
- What business outcomes do we want? (growth, cost savings, faster time-to-market, improved customer experience, reduced risk)
- What pain points exist today? (manual processes, tech debt, outages, poor performance)
- What are the stakeholders' priorities and constraints?

## Activities

### 1. Stakeholder Interviews and Workshops
**Stakeholders to engage:**
- [ ] Business leaders/executives
- [ ] Product managers
- [ ] Engineering leads
- [ ] Operations teams
- [ ] End users/customers
- [ ] IT/Infrastructure teams

**Interview questions framework:**
- What are your top 3 business priorities for the next 12-18 months?
- What current technology limitations are blocking these priorities?
- How would you measure success for a modernization effort?
- What are your biggest concerns about changing the current system?
- What budget/timeline constraints do we need to consider?

### 2. Business Process Mapping
**Process areas to examine:**
- [ ] Customer-facing workflows
- [ ] Internal operational processes
- [ ] Integration touchpoints
- [ ] Data flows and dependencies
- [ ] Compliance/regulatory requirements

**Metrics to capture:**
- [ ] Current performance baselines
- [ ] Business impact of system downtime
- [ ] Time spent on manual workarounds
- [ ] Cost of current maintenance
- [ ] Customer satisfaction scores

### 3. Strategic Alignment Assessment
**Business drivers to evaluate:**
- [ ] Market competitive pressures
- [ ] Regulatory/compliance requirements
- [ ] Growth/scaling needs
- [ ] Cost optimization targets
- [ ] Innovation/differentiation goals
- [ ] Risk mitigation priorities

## Expected Outputs

### Business Context Document
**Problem Statement:**
- Current state challenges
- Business impact of these challenges
- Urgency and timing considerations

**Desired Outcomes:**
- Primary business objectives
- Success criteria and KPIs
- Stakeholder expectations
- Constraints and non-negotiables

**Strategic Context:**
- How modernization aligns with business strategy
- Competitive landscape considerations
- Market timing factors

### Success Metrics Framework
**Quantitative KPIs:**
- [ ] Revenue impact targets
- [ ] Cost reduction goals
- [ ] Performance improvement targets
- [ ] Time-to-market improvements
- [ ] Operational efficiency gains

**Qualitative Measures:**
- [ ] Customer experience improvements
- [ ] Developer productivity enhancements
- [ ] System reliability/stability
- [ ] Maintainability and flexibility
- [ ] Security and compliance posture

## Completion Checklist
- [ ] All key stakeholders interviewed
- [ ] Business processes mapped and documented
- [ ] Current pain points quantified
- [ ] Success criteria clearly defined
- [ ] Strategic alignment confirmed
- [ ] Initial budget/timeline parameters established
- [ ] Business context document reviewed and approved

## Next Steps
Upon completion, proceed to **Phase 2: Assess** to understand the technical reality of the current system.
