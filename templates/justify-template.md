# Phase 3: Justify (Risk + ROI Analysis / Business Case)

## Goal
Answer the "why now" question and secure approval for modernization investment.

## Key Questions
- Why should we modernize? (risk reduction, cost savings, strategic enablement)
- What's the expected ROI and business impact?
- What are the risks of doing nothing?

### 3. Risk Assessment
**Risks of NOT Modernizing:**
- [ ] **Security Risks:** Vulnerability to attacks, compliance failures
- [ ] **Business Risks:** Inability to scale, lost competitive advantage
- [ ] **Technical Risks:** System failures, unsupported technology
- [ ] **Financial Risks:** Escalating maintenance costs, emergency fixes
- [ ] **Operational Risks:** Developer turnover, knowledge loss

**Risks of Modernizing:**
- [ ] **Execution Risks:** Project delays, budget overruns
- [ ] **Technical Risks:** Migration issues, performance degradation
- [ ] **Business Risks:** Temporary disruption, user experience impact
- [ ] **Resource Risks:** Team capacity, skill gaps

## Expected Outputs

### Business Case Document
**Executive Summary:** One-page overview for leadership decision-making

**Detailed Analysis:**
- Current state costs and risks
- Modernization benefits and ROI
- Implementation options and recommendations
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies

### Decision-Ready Presentation
**Slide Deck Structure:**
1. Current State Challenges (business impact)
2. Modernization Opportunity (strategic value)
3. Financial Analysis (costs, benefits, ROI)
4. Risk Assessment (doing nothing vs. modernizing)
5. Implementation Options (phased approaches)
6. Recommendation and Next Steps

### Go/No-Go Decision Framework
**Go Criteria (all must be met):**
- [ ] Positive ROI within acceptable timeframe
- [ ] Acceptable risk profile
- [ ] Strategic alignment with business goals
- [ ] Available resources and budget
- [ ] Executive sponsorship and support

**No-Go Criteria (any triggers rejection):**
- [ ] Negative or insufficient ROI
- [ ] Unacceptable risk exposure
- [ ] Lack of strategic alignment
- [ ] Insufficient resources or budget
- [ ] Lack of executive support

## Completion Checklist
- [ ] Current system costs fully quantified
- [ ] Modernization investment estimated
- [ ] Benefits analysis completed
- [ ] Risk assessment documented
- [ ] ROI calculations validated
- [ ] Business case document prepared
- [ ] Executive presentation created
- [ ] Go/No-Go decision criteria evaluated
- [ ] Stakeholder review and approval obtained

## Decision Outcomes

### If GO: Proceed to Phase 4 (Plan)
- Secure budget and resource allocation
- Establish project governance structure
- Begin detailed modernization planning

### If NO-GO: Document and Defer
- Document decision rationale
- Establish criteria for future re-evaluation
- Consider alternative approaches (minimal updates, maintenance mode)
- Set timeline for next review cycle
